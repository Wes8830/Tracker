Welcome to Use Case Tracker!

A centralized place in which Sales, Product and Engineering teams can Create, Update & Track Use Cases associated with your Client or Product.

Sales don't have the licenses to access our Product pages. Nor do Product teams always have access to salesforce. This is a central hub for the salesforce and product teams to align feedback with items that may need to be built.
